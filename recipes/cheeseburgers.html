Clint's BBQ Smash Burgers

Home

Description:

Smashburgers are something that I have always loved. I decided to go ahead and make my own.
This is something you can rotate into your weekly meals or just make them on Holidays. Remember to toast your buns!

Ingredients:

  • Grill Weight
  • Kitchen Scale
  • Charcoal Grill
  • Olive Oil
  • 1 tbsp Salt
  • 1 tbsp Black Pepper
  • 1 tbsp Adobo
  • 1 tbsp Garlic Powder
  • Kinder's Hickory Brown Sugar BBQ Sauce
  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • Burger Buns
  • I bag of Rally's Fries

Steps:

  1. Measure the Ground Beef out in 3oz measurements
  2. Once done oil the ground beef individually
  3. Smash Burgers down with a grill weight
  4. Mix all seasonings into a bowl and sprinkle the mixture onto each patty
  5. Start Charcoal grill and get heat to 350 Degrees
  6. Once grill is ready place burgers and buns on the grill
  7. Remove buns when ready and cook for 15 minutes
  8. Use the grill press to flatten again and flip burgers
  9. Continue to cook burgers for another 15 minutes
  10. Place barbecue sauce and cheese on top
  11. Cook until melted. Remove and dress your burgers
  12. Follow instructions for Rally's fries
  13. Remove from the Air Fryer and enjoy!

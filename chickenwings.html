Clint's Air Fryed Creole Wings

Home

Description:

Growing up in New Orleans I got used to a little bit of spice. As a child, I moved around a lot and I could not find a lot of foods that pleased my palette.
As a result, I use to add spice to all types of food and one result is my favorite way to make chicken wings. With a little spice, and a lot of flavor my famous wings were born.
I hope you enjoy these wings as much as I do. A little reminder from home is always a good thing.

Ingredients:

  • Olive Oil
  • 1 tbsp Salt
  • 1 tbsp Black Pepper
  • 1 tbsp Adobo
  • 1 tbsp Garlic Powder
  • Kinder's Hickory Brown Sugar BBQ Sauce

Steps:

  1. Cut the wings into flats and drums
  2. Coat wings in Olive Oil and season to taste
  3. Preheat Air Fryer to 360 Degrees
  4. Once Air Fryer is preheated, cook wings for 18 minutes
  5. Flip wings every six minutes
  6. Once done remove wings from Air Fryer
  7. Preheat Air Fryer to 390 Degrees
  8. Once preheated cook wings for 7 minutes. Do not flip.
  9. Remove from the Air Fryer and enjoy!
